Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Quantity: 12 pancakes
Ingredient Amount:
All Purpose Flour 1 1/2 cups
Baking Soda 3 1/4 teaspoons
Salt 1 1/2 cups
White Sugar 1 tablespoon
Milk 1 1/4 cups
Egg 1
Butter 3 tablespoons, melted

Step 1: Mix dry ingredients first. In another bowl, whisk eggs and milk together, and pour that mixture into your dry ingredients.

Step 2:Spray pan with oil or butter down the sides. Then pour a small amount of batter in the middle, about a fourth of a cup. Brown on both sides. Enjoy!

Three Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Quantity: 12 cookies
Ingredients Amount
Peanut Butter 1 cups
Sugar 1 cups
Egg 1

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Stir ingredients together until smooth. Roll into even 1-inch balls or use a small ice cream scoop. Put parchment on baking sheet and place each cookie on sheet in rows. Then spray a fork with vegetable oil and press down on the dough twice, making the unique cross pattern on the top. Do this to every cookie.

Step 2: Place baking sheet in the oven at 350 degrees. Bake for 12 minutes. Store in an airtight container.

Easy Mac And Cheese

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Quantity: 8 servings
Ingredients Amount
Any Kind of Noodle (Usually Elbow Macaroni) 8 oz.
All Purpose Flour 2 tablespoons
Butter 2 tablespoons
Sea Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Garlic Powder (optional) 1/4 teaspoon
Whole Milk 1 cup
Sour Cream (or Greek Yogurt) 1/4 cup
Shredded Cheese 2 cups

Step 1: Cook noodles in a saucepan using package instructions.

Step 2: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Add flour, garlic powder and sea salt when melted. Then cook for 1 minute, or until slightly brown.

Step 3: Add milk and whisk together until smooth. Then add sour cream (or greek yogurt) and whisk until smooth.

Step 4: Cook on medium high heat until mixture is thickened (about 2-5 minutes). Do not let boil.

Step 5: Once mixture is thick (sticks to the back of spatula), reduce heat to low and add cheese. Mix until cheese is melted and mixture is smooth. Add more salt, cheese or garlic powder as you like.

Step 6: Add noodles to cheese sauce and stir until cheese is evenly distributed.

Step 7: Let macaroni and cheese sit in the sauce for 2 minutes or until cheese sauce has thickened and sticks to the noodles. Serve warm!
